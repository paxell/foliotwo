Queensland Art Gallery |
Gallery of Modern Art

The gallery needed an interactive solution for wayfinding that showcased both of the gallery's buildings.

The gallery maps gather data from the website calendar to provide up-do date information on exhibitions.

Highlighting exhibitions

The map pins and exhibition listing update automatically from the exhibitions calendar.

Gallery services

Services such as the gallery's restaurants and shops are also shown on the maps.

Two buildings

On the desktop view the two gallery buildings are shown side by side.

Multiple floors

Views of all floors are available via a drop-down, defaulting to the entrance level.
