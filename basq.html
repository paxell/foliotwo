Botanical Art Queensland

A new website was required to showcase artists' work and allow members to add artist profiles and update event information.

I was also asked to refresh the logo and I created an accompanying style guide to outline the treatment of printed materials.

Artist profiles

A new feature for this website redevelopment was the addition of artists' profiles. Image galleries throughout helped to showcase the artists' striking work.

CMS implementation

The website was developed as a CMS using Drupal, and training was provided, to allow members to update content and add artists' profiles and images easily.

Style guide

A style guide was created to outline the treatment of promotional materials and gallery signage as well as a stationery suite.

Style guide

The Society create their own promotional materials, so templates were provided to ensure consistency with the new branding.
